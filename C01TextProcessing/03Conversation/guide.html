About

How it Works Watson Conversation allows you to quickly build, test and deploy a bot or virtual agent across mobile devices, messaging platforms like Slack or even on a physical robot. Conversation has a visual dialog builder to help you create natural conversations between your apps and users, without any coding experience required. Intended use
  • Build Facebook, Twitter, Slack and other messaging and social network platform bots that let users interact instantly
  • Add a chatbot to your website that can respond to frequently asked questions from customers
  • Allow customers to control your mobile app using natural language virtual agents
Your Input
  • An idea to help your users--whether it's answering questions, getting feedback or following a procedure
  • Some time to train Watson about the specifics of your industry with Conversation's easy-to-use web app
Service Output
  • After some training and configuration, your first bot is ready to start interacting with users
  • You can create a bot powered by natural language understanding in minutes, not months
